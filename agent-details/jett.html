JETT

// ROLE
Duelist

// BIOGRAPHY

Representing her home country of South Korea, Jett's agile and evasive fighting style lets her take risks no one else can. She runs circles around every skirmish, cutting enemies up before they even know what hit them.

// ABILITIES

Q - UPDRAFT

INSTANTLY propel Jett high into the air.

E - TAILWIND

INSTANTLY propel Jett in the direction she is moving. If Jett is standing still, she will propel forward.

C - CLOUDBURST

INSTANTLY throw a projectile that expands into a brief vision-blocking cloud on impact with a surface.

X - BLADE STORM (Ultimate)

EQUIP a set of highly accurate throwing knives. FIRE to throw a single knife at your target. ALT FIRE to throw all remaining daggers at your target. Getting a kill restores all daggers.
