ISO

// ROLE
Duelist

// BIOGRAPHY

Chinese fixer for hire, Iso goes into a flow state to dismantle the opposition. Reconfiguring ambient energy into bulletproof protection, he proceeds with focus towards his next duel to the death.

// ABILITIES

Q - UNDERCUT

EQUIP a molecular bolt. FIRE to throw it forward, applying a brief FRAGILE to all players it touches. The bolt can pass through solid objects, including walls.

E - DOUBLE TAP

START a focus timer. Once complete, enter a flow state during which downed enemies you kill or damage generate an energy orb. Shooting this orb grants you a shield that absorbs one instance of damage from any source.

C - CONTINGENCY

EQUIP to assemble prismatic energy. FIRE to push an indestructible wall of energy forward that blocks bullets.

X - KILL CONTRACT (Ultimate)

EQUIP an interdimensional arena. FIRE to hurl a column of energy through the battlefield, pulling you and the first enemy hit into the arena. You and your opponent duel to the death.
