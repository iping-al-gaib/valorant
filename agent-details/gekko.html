GEKKO

// ROLE
Initiator

// BIOGRAPHY

Gekko the Angeleno leads a tight-knit crew of calamitous creatures. His buddies bound forward, scattering enemies out of the way, with Gekko chasing them down to plant or defuse the Spike.

// ABILITIES

Q - WINGMAN

EQUIP Wingman. FIRE to send Wingman forward seeking enemies. Wingman unleashes a concussive blast toward the first enemy he sees. ALT FIRE when targeting a Spike site or planted Spike to have Wingman plant or defuse the Spike.

E - DIZZY

EQUIP Dizzy. FIRE to send Dizzy soaring forward through the air. Dizzy charges then unleashes plasma blasts at enemies in line of sight. Enemies hit by her plasma are blinded.

C - MOSH PIT

EQUIP Mosh. FIRE to throw Mosh like a grenade. ALT FIRE to throw underhand. After landing Mosh duplicates across a large area then after a short delay explodes.
