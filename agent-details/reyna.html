REYNA

// ROLE
Duelist

// BIOGRAPHY

Forged in the heart of Mexico, Reyna dominates single combat, popping off with each kill she scores. Her capability is only limited by her raw skill, making her highly dependent on performance.

// ABILITIES

Q - DEVOUR

Enemies killed by Reyna leave behind Soul Orbs that last 3 seconds. INSTANTLY consume a nearby soul orb, rapidly healing for a short duration. Health gained through this skill exceeding 100 will decay over time.

E - DISMISS

INSTANTLY consume a nearby soul orb, becoming intangible for a short duration. If EMPRESS is active, also become invisible.

C - LEER

EQUIP a destructible ethereal eye. ACTIVATE to cast the eye a short distance forward. The eye will Nearsight all enemies who look at it.

X - EMPRESS (Ultimate)

INSTANTLY enter a frenzy, increasing firing speed, equip and reload speed. Scoring a kill renews the duration. While active, Devour is cast automatically and does not consume the orb.
