FRACTURE

// LOCATION
New Mexico, USA

// OVERVIEW

A top-secret research facility split apart by a failed radianite experiment. Fracture's unique H-shape layout places the Defenders' spawn in the middle, allowing Attackers to assault sites from two different directions simultaneously.

// UNIQUE FEATURE

Ziplines: A network of ziplines runs beneath the map, connecting the front and back of the Attacker spawns. This allows for fast, silent rotations across the entire map, creating opportunities for aggressive flanks and pinches.
