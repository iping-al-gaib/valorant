SPLIT

// LOCATION
Tokyo, Japan

// OVERVIEW

A map defined by its strong verticality and tight chokepoints. Control of the central Mid area is paramount, as it provides access to both bomb sites from elevated positions (Vents and Rafters). Teamwork is essential to break through defensive setups.

// UNIQUE FEATURE

Ropes (Ascenders): Ropes are located in Mid and A Heavens, allowing for rapid vertical movement. They enable quick rotations, aggressive peeks from unexpected angles, and fast escapes, but players are vulnerable while using them.
