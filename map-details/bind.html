BIND

// LOCATION
Rabat, Morocco

// OVERVIEW

Bind is a unique map with two bomb sites and no middle lane. Instead, it features two one-way teleporters that allow for extremely fast rotations between sites, leading to dynamic attack strategies and unpredictable defensive setups.

// UNIQUE FEATURE

Teleporters: Players can instantly travel from A Short to B Short, and from B Long to A Lobby. Entering a teleporter is loud, alerting enemies to the rotation, but it opens up possibilities for quick fakes and retakes.
